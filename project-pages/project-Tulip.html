PWA Project: Tulip - a modern dating platform

Tulip will be a modern dating web app, which built upon the robust and versatile MERN stack

Tulip combines the power of MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js to create a seamless user experience.

Project Details

What sets Tulip apart from the crowd is its innovative approach to matchmaking, driven by a specialized algorithm. This algorithm is designed to connect individuals who are genuinely seeking long-term and meaningful relationships.

When it comes to technology, the use of the MERN stack ensures scalability, flexibility, and reliability in our infrastructure, while also enabling real-time communication. The integration of Firebase or Heroku further enhances the platform's performance, ensuring that users can interact smoothly and efficiently. With Tulip, we aim to provide a responsive and feature-rich experience, where user profiles, messaging, and personalized preferences are just a click away.

In addition, every aspect of Tulip's design, from profile creation to the messaging experience, has been carefully thought out to provide users with an enjoyable and safe environment for their dating journey
