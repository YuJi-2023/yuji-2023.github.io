React Project: KidsKnow
Kids Know is targeted to be a job management web app powered by ReactJS, specially crafted for young users embracing self-directed study.
Project Details

Kids Know streamlines the process of creating tasks and managing study calendars, offering a user-friendly, dynamic solution for students to take control of their learning journey.

With ReactJS at its core, Kids Know is set to optimise how young learners approach self-directed study, ensuring a seamless and engaging experience.
